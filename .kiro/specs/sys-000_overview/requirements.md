# System Requirements (sys-000)

## Scope
- Solder smoke absorber with speed control
- Bench-top use, USB-C power (tentative)

## Requirements

### SYS-001 Physical Safety
ID: SYS-001  
Verification: OPS-900-TST-001  

The system shall include a method to prevent accidental contact with the fan blades.

### SYS-002 Electrical Safety
ID: SYS-002  
Verification: TBD  

The system shall include safety measures/precautions as device regularly used desktop. Eg.(Overheat/Short/Excessive current/damaging Powersource)

### SYS-003 Fume capture effectiveness
ID: SYS-003
Verification: HW-XX (TBD)

The system shall capture visible solder smoke when the nozzle is placed within 15 cm of the solder joint under defined test conditions.(Tentative)

### SYS-004 Noise
ID: SYS-004  
Verification: HW-XX (TBD)

The system offers acceptable for indoor desktop use.
quantitative dBA target to be defined after prototype.

### SYS-005 Power budget
ID: SYS-005  
Verification: HW-XX (TBD)

The system shall operate from USB-C 5V input and not exceed 5 W (Tentative).

### SYS-006 Filteration
ID: SYS-006  
Verification: TBD

The system shall offer patricle filteration. The filter must be easy to exchange and shall be generic product so that maintenance cost will be minimum.(eg.generic vent unwoven filters)

### SYS-007 Dimension
ID: SYS-007
Verification: TBD

The system shall remain suitable for desktop use.

### SYS-008 Local capture
ID: SYS-008
Verification: TBD
The system shall support local fume capture via a nozzle or inlet that can be positioned near solder joint.

### SYS-009 Speed control
ID: SYS-009
Verification: TBD
The system shall allow user to adjust the fan speed(at least 3 descrete level, such as OFF-LOW-MID-HIGH)

### SYS-010 Auto-off
ID: SYS-010
Verification: TBD

The system shall automatically stop after a configurable period of inactivity (30 minutes) to prevent being left on unintentionally.

### SYS-011 Maintenability
ID: SYS-011  
Verification: TBD

The system shall allow users to change filter without any tools.
(preferably one-touch)
